## Specifications [#specifications]

[Self-Serve Integration Onboarding (SSIO) Flow](https://developer.doordash.com/en-US/docs/marketplace/overview/onboarding/ssio/)

SSIO is required for all middleware platform integrations.

[Store Onboarding Webhook (SOW) Flow](https://developer.doordash.com/en-US/docs/marketplace/overview/onboarding/sow/)

The SOW flow is available for single-merchant integrations only. Middleware platform integrations must build SSIO.

[Direct to Merchant | Enterprise Partner Manager Flow](https://developer.doordash.com/en-US/docs/marketplace/overview/onboarding/epm/)

The Direct to Merchant | EPM flow is available for single-merchant integrations only. This is an acceptable alternative to Store Onboarding Webhook flow if the integration meets the outlined EPM flow criteria.
